Urban Commune – A Serene Open Plots Community Amidst Nature

For those looking to build their dream home in a green, peaceful, and future-ready location, Urban Commune offers the perfect opportunity. Spread across 100 acres in Narayankhed, this DTCP-approved open plot project is designed for nature lovers, investors, and future homeowners.

Project Highlights

  • 100-acre DTCP-approved gated community
  • Spacious & well-planned open plots
  • Pollution-free, nature-friendly environment
  • Well-connected to major highways and cities
  • Future-ready investment with high appreciation potential

Why Choose Urban Commune?

Urban Commune offers large, well-planned plots in a secure, gated environment with wide roads and lush green landscapes. Designed for sustainable and peaceful living, the project includes:

  • Landscaped open spaces & tree-lined avenues
  • Jogging & reflexology walking tracks for wellness
  • Children’s play area for fun and safety
  • Community clubhouse with leisure facilities
  • Eco-friendly infrastructure with green belt areas

Investment Potential & Future Growth

Urban Commune is located in an area with huge investment potential, making it an ideal choice for those looking to buy land for future development.

  • Excellent road connectivity to Hyderabad & nearby towns
  • Fast-growing real estate corridor with high appreciation value
  • Ideal for both long-term investment & immediate construction

Whether you're looking to build a dream home in a peaceful environment or secure a high-value investment, Urban Commune offers an unbeatable opportunity in Hyderabad’s expanding real estate market.
